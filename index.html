FAQ

Common questions

What types of networks do you work with?

We specialize in Ubiquiti Unifi but work with a wide range of equipment. Whether it's a home mesh system, a multi-site business network, or a legacy setup that needs upgrading, we can help.

Do you offer remote support?

Yes. Many network issues can be diagnosed and resolved remotely through your Unifi controller or a secure screen-share session. We serve clients nationwide.

How does pricing work?

We offer simple, transparent hourly rates starting at $200/hr. You can also save with 2-hour or 5-hour blocks. No hidden fees, no surprise charges. See our pricing page for details.

How quickly can you start?

Typically within 1–3 business days of your first inquiry, depending on current availability. Book a free discovery call and we'll get you on the schedule.

What does a typical engagement look like?

It starts with a free 30-minute discovery call to understand your needs. From there, we scope the work, schedule time, and get to it. You'll receive a full summary of everything done, along with recommendations and next steps.
