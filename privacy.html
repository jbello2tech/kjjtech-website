Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

KJJ Tech ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at www.kjjtech.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. By using our site, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways — information you provide directly, and information collected automatically when you visit our site.

Information you provide:

  • Name, email address, and phone number submitted through our contact form
  • Payment information submitted through Stripe when purchasing a service block (we do not store card details — these are handled entirely by Stripe)

Information collected automatically:

  • Pages visited, time spent on site, and general location (country/region) via Google Analytics
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Referring URL (how you found our site)

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries submitted through our contact form
  • To process and fulfill service purchases made through Stripe
  • To understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it (via Google Analytics)
  • To communicate with you about your service sessions or account

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process your data:

  • Formspree — handles contact form submissions and forwards them to our email. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — processes all payments securely. We never see or store your card details. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics — tracks anonymous usage data to help us improve the site. See Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties. We only share your data with the service providers listed above, and only to the extent necessary to operate our business.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in our email inbox for as long as needed to respond and follow up. Payment records are retained as required by law and Stripe's policies. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics' default retention settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct or delete your personal information
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@kjjtech.com.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies only through Google Analytics to track anonymous usage. We do not use advertising cookies or sell browsing data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
