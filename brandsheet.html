Voice & Tone

How KJJ Tech Communicates

The brand voice is confident but approachable, technical but never jargon-heavy. We speak like a knowledgeable friend who happens to be an expert.

Do

  • Be direct, clear, and honest
  • Use plain language over technical jargon
  • Explain the "why" behind recommendations
  • Keep pricing transparent and upfront
  • Speak with confidence, not arrogance

Don't

  • Hide pricing or create artificial urgency
  • Use overly salesy or pushy language
  • Overcomplicate explanations
  • Make promises that can't be backed up
  • Use walls of jargon or acronyms without context
